the questions

awake lipo, answered honestly.

Am I really awake the whole time?

Yes — and that's the point. We use local anesthesia to fully numb the treatment area, so you're comfortable, present, and in control. You can talk to us, listen to music, even watch. No general anesthesia means no breathing tube, no grogginess, and no lost day.

Will it hurt?

The first numbing injections feel like a pinch and some pressure — that's the most you'll feel. During sculpting, patients describe pressure and movement, not sharp pain. And because you're awake, you can tell us how you're feeling in real time and we adjust immediately.

How long does the procedure take?

Most procedures run about 90 minutes; smaller single areas can be quicker and larger plans like SLEEQUE360™ can take longer. Either way, you walk in and walk out the same day — no hospital, no overnight stay.

Is awake liposuction safe?

Awake tumescent liposuction avoids general anesthesia entirely, which removes some of the most significant risks of traditional surgery. Every procedure is performed by Dr. Jackson himself, and every patient is screened at their consult to confirm they're a good candidate.

When can I go back to work?

Many desk-job patients are back within 1–3 days. You'll wear a compression garment for several weeks, and light workouts typically return around weeks 3–4. Your consult maps your exact timeline.

How much does it cost?

Every plan is custom to your body and goals, so exact pricing happens at your consult — and monthly payment plans are available through CareCredit. Applying takes about a minute and you’ll have your decision right away.
